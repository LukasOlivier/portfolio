Monopoly

Front-end & back-end

The final project of our first year in computer science was a playable web-version of the popular game Monopoly.

We front-end was made with vanilla HTML, CSS and JS. The back-end was made using Java

We worked in groups of 5 people and used Scrum methods to keep our workflow optimal

We also learned to work with issues, milestones and version control in general using Gitlab
